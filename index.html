Who We Are

Where Industry Meets Academic Rigour

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk. We develop practitioners equipped for high-stakes, regulated environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

IDR uniquely bridges academic insight — through our partnership with a leading UK university — and hands-on, real-world projects. Our graduates don't just learn frameworks; they apply them to live industry challenges, emerging standards, and next-generation AI risk scenarios.
